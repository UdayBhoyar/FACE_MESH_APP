================================================================================
                              BACKGROUND MODE
================================================================================

Voice commands work even when the app is in the background!

✅ Notification shown: "Voice Commands Active"
✅ Always listening for your commands
✅ Stop anytime via notification button

To stop voice commands:
  • Option 1: Open app and toggle "Voice ON" to "Voice OFF"
  • Option 2: Use notification → Tap "Stop Voice Commands"

================================================================================
                                  TIPS
================================================================================

🎤 SPEAKING TIPS:
  • Speak clearly in a normal voice
  • No need to shout or whisper
  • Works best in quiet environments
  • Natural language supported

📱 APP TIPS:
  • Most common apps are recognized
  • Can open any installed app by name
  • Try app's full name if short name doesn't work
  • Long-press "Voice Commands" button for help dialog

🔧 TROUBLESHOOTING:
  • Not working? Check microphone permission
  • Toggle voice commands off and on to restart
  • Ensure accessibility service is enabled
  • Check notification to confirm service is running
